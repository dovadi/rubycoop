**Membership fee**

> The membership fee is €200,- per year.

**Tarifs**

> The tarif for a customer is  €60,- per hour (excluding VAT).
> A member can charge the co-op for €50,- per hour (excluding VAT).

**Profit distribution**

> Once a year 50 percent of the profit is evenly distributed among the members. The general assembly can decide on the top 50 percent of the profit if the liquidity/cash (? what is the correct term) is above the threshold of €1000,- per member.
